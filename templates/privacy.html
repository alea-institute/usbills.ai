Privacy Notice

This notice explains how the ALEA Institute Inc. ("we" or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you visit usbills.ai (the "site"), an informational website to help the public better understand federal legislation. If you have any questions about this privacy notice or our data practices, please contact us at legal@aleainstitute.ai.

Information We Collect

To operate this site, we collect only:

Contact Information (Optional)

  • Name and email if you choose to contact us

Technical Information (Automatic)

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Time zone and location
  • Device operating system and platform

We do not collect sensitive personal data, information about children under 16, or use cookies on our site.

If we inadvertently collect data from children under 16, we will delete it promptly. Parents or legal guardians who believe we may have collected such information should contact us at legal@aleainstitute.ai.

How and Why We Use Your Data

We will only process your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will process your personal data in the following circumstances:

For communications. For example, responding to an email that you send to us.
Legal basis: consent

To gather analysis or valuable information. For example, analyzing which bills are most frequently read.
Legal basis: legitimate interest

For security and technical issues. For example, monitoring site usage to detect and prevent a denial of service attack.
Legal basis: legitimate interest

For legal and regulatory compliance. For example, maintaining records for tax purposes.
Legal basis: legal obligation
